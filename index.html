Our Company

At Allmark Properties, Inc. we take pride in our history, our accomplishments and our enduring relationships with investors, residents and colleagues. Allmark has been serving the needs of residents and investment partners since 1971, from practical acquisition transactions that create long-term value for our investment partners to the hands-on management of your investment. We provide a wide array of resources and support to deliver a diverse range of investment services concentrating in Southern California, Texas and Oklahoma. The Allmark management team represents a broad spectrum of real estate experience to deliver consistent, positive performance results for our partners.

Serving our residents
and investors since 1971

Stability in any company is important. We are fortunate to have several long-term colleagues, many of them acknowledged with industry awards and accolades over the years. They have been carefully chosen and receive ongoing support and training to strengthen their skills and to keep customer relations a priority.

It is our goal to find the most profitable partnerships and opportunities for our investors. Today’s investments should have positive cash flow, integrated risk protection and the ability to outperform in a slow growth economy. We are personally involved with all aspects of the business from property selection, acquisition, due diligence, the creation of the partnerships, capital formation and the actual hands-on property management. When you invest with Allmark, you get a seasoned team of industry specialists who are focused and committed to delivering returns that can actually make a difference in your future. We are committed to protecting your investment capital and strive to earn an above average rate of return on your investments.

Allmark is a proud member of the Apartment Association of the Greater Inland Empire (AAGIE), the California Apartment Association (CAA), the Institute of Real Estate Management (IREM), The National Apartment Association (NAA), The Texas Apartment Association (TAA), and the Oklahoma State Apartment Association (OSAA).

Whether you are a prospective resident or a potential investor, we hope you give us the opportunity to Exceed Your Expectations.

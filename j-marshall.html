J Marshall Square

Oklahoma City, OK

(405) 478-3260

Visit Website

J Marshall Square offers a unique uptown living experience with 280 exquisite townhomes based on the Big House™ concept. Designed to incorporate all the convenience of an apartment without losing the amenities of a house. The Big House™ concept offers residents spacious townhomes without the hassle of a mortgage or maintenance. Built on the original site of the John Marshall High School – north of Nichols Hills, the Western District and near Classen Curve. Our stylish one- and two-bedroom town homes provide direct access garages, private stairs, beautiful balconies, energy-efficient, granite countertops, and a living experience like none other. Our floor plans range from 753-1291 sq. ft. with deluxe, spacious interiors and all the comforts of home.
